KEITH TAN

Bachelor of Architecture
Senior Lecturer at Singapore Polytechnic (SP)
School of Architecture and the Built Environment (ABE)

BIO

Born in Singapore, Keith graduated from RMIT University, Melbourne, Australia (1999-2003). During his studies as well as being an architect in the industry, he has won international architectural competitions and received recognitions from the University and the company that he represented.

Fueled by passion and desire for innovation, Keith embarked on the journey as an educator on November 2008. Besides being a lecturer, Keith is spearheading the Diploma in Architecture (DARCH) Fablab, specialising in Parametric Designs and Rapid Prototyping. Currently, Keith is also involved in supporting ABE as a multi-media designer and webmaster.
